## Story

As a lid,
I want Afrikaans lifecycle emails around my term,
so that I'm thanked on activation and warned before expiry. (FR-9a, R5)

**The convergent finding (all three layers, independently): the in-place renewal gap.** At launch (Story 4.5) a "renew" re-buys a fixed term via the 4.2 PayFast hand-off, and WC Memberships EXTENDS the end date of the **still-`active`** membership â€” typically WITHOUT a status transition. The original code only (re)scheduled on a transition INTO active (`old !== active && new === active`), so an in-place renewal never rescheduled: the OLD anchor's warning survived and would fire a false "expires in 1 week" to a member who renewed months out. Toggles are OFF today so no live mail sends, but this had to be correct before 4.8's copy is enabled. Three independent reviewers (Blind Hunter, Edge Case Hunter, Acceptance Auditor) converged on it â€” confirmed real, now fixed by FIX 1â€“3 together. The AC-3 / Completion-Note claim that a send-time re-check alone "denies the renewed member" was an **overclaim** (it described a renewed member as one whose end date moved *past* the window â€” the opposite of a renewed-forward member) â€” corrected below + in the Completion Notes.
